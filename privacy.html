Privacy Policy

How Trillions handles information submitted through this website.

Last updated: 25 June 2026

Information we collect

When you contact Trillions, we may collect the information you submit, including your name, email address, inquiry type, product details, supplier documents, and message content.

How we use information

We use submitted information to review inquiries, respond to requests, compare supplier information, coordinate sourcing discussions, and improve our services.

AI assistant use

If the AI assistant is enabled, messages you submit may be processed by an AI service provider for the purpose of generating a response. Do not submit passwords, payment card details, private keys, or highly sensitive personal information through the chat.

Sharing

We may share necessary inquiry details with suppliers, logistics providers, consultants, or service providers only when required to evaluate or process your request.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No online system can guarantee absolute security.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Trillions through the contact page.
