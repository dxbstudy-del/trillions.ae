Privacy Policy

How Trillions handles information submitted through this website.

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Information we collect

When you contact Trillions, submit an RFQ, request supplier checks, compare quotations, request private-label support, ask for import-readiness review, or register as a supplier, we may collect the information you submit, including contact details, company details, supplier links, quotations, product photos, catalogs, certificates, packing details, and message content.

How we use information

We use submitted information to review inquiries, prepare supplier questions, compare supplier information, evaluate supplier registrations, coordinate sourcing discussions, and respond to requests.

Files and attachments

This static website does not upload files directly. Files may be shared by email or WhatsApp. Do not send passwords, private keys, payment card details, or highly sensitive personal information.

Supplier registrations

Supplier registration information may be organized internally by country, city, product categories, MOQ, lead time, payment terms, export markets, certificates, catalog link, website, WhatsApp, and contact person. Registration does not guarantee verification, endorsement, approval, buyer orders, or public listing.

Sharing

We may share necessary inquiry details with suppliers, logistics providers, consultants, or service providers only when required to evaluate or process your request.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No online system can guarantee absolute security.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Trillions through the contact page or email info@trillions.ae.

This page is a starter policy and should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before final commercial launch.
