Private-label checklist

Clarify the product before suppliers quote.

Product specification sheet

Materials, dimensions, features, colors, accessories, target use, quality expectations, warranty, and product files.

Logo and packaging requirements

Logo method, packaging type, label language, barcode, insert cards, gift box, carton mark, and retail display needs.

Sample request

Sample cost, sample lead time, refund policy, logo sample, packaging sample, delivery method, and approval steps.

MOQ by product, color, packaging, logo method, scent, size, or SKU bundle, plus first-order flexibility.
