What Trillions reviews

Supplier evidence, payment risk, quote completeness, and identity consistency.

Supplier red flags

  • Pressure to pay quickly or outside agreed channels.
  • Copied product photos or inconsistent company names.
  • Weak supplier profile, vague address, or unclear business type.

Missing documents

  • Business license, product certificates, test reports, catalog, packing data, or warranty terms.
  • Certificates that do not match the product, market, or supplier name.

Payment-risk questions

  • Deposit structure, bank account name, payment terms, sample policy, refund terms, and written supplier commitments.

Quotation completeness

  • MOQ, unit price, specification, lead time, packing, incoterms, warranty, sample cost, and what is excluded.

Supplier identity consistency

  • Company name, website, email domain, bank account name, document names, factory address, and marketplace profile.

Factory vs trading-company signs

  • Production focus, factory evidence, product range breadth, customization answers, and document ownership.

Clear limits

Supplier check is a risk review, not a guarantee.
