RFQ checklist

Details that help suppliers quote properly.

Product details needed

Product function, photos, links, model, materials, dimensions, color, accessories, quality expectations, and target buyer.

Quantity and MOQ

Target order quantity, expected monthly demand, sample needs, minimum acceptable MOQ, and color or SKU split.

Target price

Budget, target unit price, pricing assumptions, currency, incoterms preference, and what should be included.

Destination market

UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, or other market, with any climate, label, language, or retail needs.

Packaging requirements

Retail packaging, private label, carton details, barcode, manual, inserts, Arabic/English labels, and gift-box needs.

Certificate and sample request

Certificate questions, test reports, sample cost, sample refund, sample delivery, warranty, and inspection needs.
