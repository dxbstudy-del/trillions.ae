Example workflows

Example case studies without naming real suppliers.

These are sample workflows that show how Trillions can structure a review. They do not describe real clients or real suppliers.

Example: Supplier Red-Flag Review

Input: Supplier link, low unit price, urgent payment request, and product photo.

Review: Company identity consistency, quotation gaps, missing documents, sample policy, payment-risk questions.

Output: Red-flag notes and supplier questions before payment.

Example: Quotation Comparison

Input: Three quotes for the same product with different MOQ, packing, and warranty terms.

Review: Unit price, MOQ, lead time, payment terms, packing, warranty, and missing details.

Output: Comparison notes and negotiation questions.

Example: Private Label Request

Input: Brand concept, reference product, packaging ideas, and target market.

Review: Spec sheet, sample request, logo method, packaging checklist, MOQ questions.

Output: Supplier-ready private-label brief.

Example: Import Readiness Review

Input: Invoice draft, packing details, certificate claims, and shipping terms.

Review: Packing list, certificate questions, HS-code questions, labels, regulated-product warnings.

Output: Import-readiness gaps for logistics or compliance follow-up.

Example: RFQ Preparation

Input: Product photo, target quantity, target price, destination, and packaging needs.

Review: Product requirements, MOQ, target price, certificate needs, sample request, supplier questions.

Output: Structured RFQ for supplier outreach.
