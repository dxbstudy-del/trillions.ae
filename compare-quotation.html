Comparison points

Price alone is not enough.

Unit price comparisonReview price by model, specification, quantity tier, currency, and what is included or excluded.
MOQ comparisonCompare MOQ by SKU, color, packaging, private label, sample, or first order.
Lead timeCheck sample time, production time, packing time, and shipping assumption.
Payment termsCompare deposit, balance timing, payment method, bank name consistency, and refund language.
PackingReview carton size, gross weight, units per carton, packaging cost, and label requirements.
WarrantyCompare warranty period, spare parts, defect policy, and written supplier responsibility.
