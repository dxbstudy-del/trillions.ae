What to prepare

Make your supplier profile easier to review.

Clear supplier information helps Trillions build a stronger source list and match suppliers to buyer requests more responsibly.

Company proofLegal company name, location, website, business license, factory profile, and export history.
Product proofCatalogs, photos, specifications, certificates, packaging options, samples, and customization limits.
Commercial termsMOQ, price range, lead time, payment terms, warranty, sample cost, and production capacity.
Buyer fitBest product categories, target markets, private-label ability, after-sales support, and language support.

How Trillions uses supplier registrations

A cleaner supplier intake makes sourcing faster later.

1. Collect sourcesSupplier details are organized by country, category, product focus, MOQ, lead time, documents, and contact method.
2. Review before useTrillions may check company signals, product evidence, communication quality, documents, and commercial terms.
3. Match to requestsSuitable suppliers may be considered when a buyer request matches the category, quantity, market, and product requirements.

What suppliers should expect

Registration is an intake step, not a sales guarantee.

No automatic approvalSubmitted suppliers are not automatically verified, recommended, listed, or approved for buyer orders.
Follow-up may be requestedTrillions may ask for catalogs, licenses, sample policy, certificates, product files, or clearer commercial terms.
Keep details currentPrices, MOQ, lead time, certificates, stock, and product availability should be reconfirmed before any buyer decision.
