01

Camping & Outdoor Product Sourcing

Products we help source: tents, shade products, camping lights, portable storage, cookware, vehicle camping accessories, outdoor furniture, and private-label outdoor kits.

Supplier questions to ask:
  • What materials, waterproof rating, load rating, temperature range, and warranty apply?
  • Can the supplier provide samples, spare parts, manuals, and packaging options?
Documents to request:

Product spec sheet, material details, test reports where relevant, packing list, warranty terms, and export invoice details.

Common red flags:

No material confirmation, copied product photos, vague warranty, no sample policy, or unclear carton size and weight.

MOQ and quotation checklist:

MOQ by color and model, unit price by quantity tier, lead time, packaging cost, logo cost, sample cost, and incoterms.

02

RC & Hobby Product Sourcing

Products we help source: RC cars, drones where appropriate, hobby parts, batteries, chargers, controllers, upgrade kits, spare parts, and retail bundles.

Supplier questions to ask:
  • What motor, battery, controller, charging, spare-part, and warranty details are included?
  • Can the supplier provide manuals, safety instructions, replacement parts, and after-sales support?
Documents to request:

Technical specifications, battery details, user manuals, safety information, warranty terms, and product photos or videos.

Common red flags:

Unclear battery rating, no spare parts list, no manual, weak warranty terms, or supplier cannot explain product differences.

MOQ and quotation checklist:

MOQ by model, battery inclusion, controller type, accessory cost, spare-part price, packaging, sample price, and lead time.

03

Home & Lifestyle Private Label

Products we help source: diffusers, organizers, decor, lifestyle gifts, home accessories, wellness-adjacent products, packaging sets, and retail-ready bundles.

Supplier questions to ask:
  • What materials, finishes, ingredients, scent details, size options, and packaging choices are available?
  • What logo methods, label languages, sample process, and customization limits apply?
Documents to request:

Spec sheet, safety documents where relevant, ingredient or material information, packaging dielines, label files, and sample policy.

Common red flags:

No clear material or ingredient data, inconsistent photos, packaging not confirmed, or private-label promises without sample proof.

MOQ and quotation checklist:

MOQ per SKU, logo and packaging cost, color or scent MOQ, sample cost, carton details, lead time, and payment terms.

04

Tools & Machinery Supplier Review

Products we help source: workshop tools, machinery, construction support items, machine accessories, spare parts, rental-product opportunities, and commercial equipment.

Supplier questions to ask:
  • What voltage, capacity, materials, spare parts, consumables, manuals, warranty, and service requirements apply?
  • Can the supplier confirm packing method, machine weight, installation needs, and after-sales support?
Documents to request:

Technical data sheet, manuals, warranty terms, spare parts list, packing data, certificates where relevant, and proforma invoice.

Common red flags:

No technical sheet, unclear power requirements, no spare parts, vague warranty, or certificates that do not match the product.

MOQ and quotation checklist:

MOQ or single-unit availability, accessory inclusion, spare-part pricing, packing dimensions, lead time, warranty, and incoterms.

05

Fashion Accessories Sourcing

Products we help source: bags, small accessories, packaging, display materials, gift sets, private-label accessories, and retail presentation concepts.

Supplier questions to ask:
  • What material, stitching, hardware, color options, logo method, packaging, and sample process are available?
  • Can the supplier confirm finish consistency, defect policy, and production photos before shipping?
Documents to request:

Material details, color card, hardware options, logo method samples, packaging options, quote, and packing list.

Common red flags:

Unclear materials, no real sample photos, inconsistent hardware, weak packaging detail, or MOQ changes after discussion.

MOQ and quotation checklist:

MOQ by design and color, sample cost, logo cost, packaging cost, production lead time, carton details, and payment terms.

06

Custom Product Sourcing

Products we help source: new category ideas, project-based requirements, promotional products, replacement products, and supplier opportunities that need structured review.

Supplier questions to ask:
  • What exact function, materials, dimensions, packaging, target buyer, and market requirements must be met?
  • Can suppliers provide proof of similar production, samples, documents, warranty, and clear quotation terms?
Documents to request:

Product files, supplier profile, quote, certificates where relevant, sample photos, packing details, and customization notes.

Common red flags:

Unclear supplier capability, unrealistic pricing, no proof of similar products, missing documents, or pressure to pay quickly.

MOQ and quotation checklist:

MOQ by specification, tooling or setup cost, sample timing, packaging, lead time, payment terms, and confirmation of what is included.

Clear disclaimers

Product decisions still need confirmation.

Trillions can help structure the sourcing review, but product responsibility does not end with a supplier quote or catalog photo.

Samples and inspections matterPhysical samples, quality checks, test reports, and production inspections may be needed before larger orders.
Compliance is market-specificRegulated products, labeling, safety, customs, and certificates should be checked with qualified local specialists.
Supplier terms can changeMOQ, price, lead time, packaging, warranty, and shipping assumptions must be reconfirmed before payment or purchase.
