Categories

Products Trillions can help review or source.

Camping & Outdoor Product Sourcing

Products: Tents, shade products, lights, storage, outdoor furniture, vehicle camping accessories.

Questions: Waterproof rating, materials, load limits, climate suitability, spare parts, warranty.

Documents: Spec sheet, test reports where relevant, packing list, warranty, invoice basics.

Red flags: Copied photos, no sample policy, vague materials, missing carton data.

Review Outdoor Supplier

RC & Hobby Product Sourcing

Products: RC cars, batteries, chargers, controllers, spare parts, upgrade kits, bundles.

Questions: Motor, battery rating, controller type, spare parts, manual, warranty, after-sales support.

Documents: Technical sheet, safety notes, manuals, product videos, warranty terms.

Red flags: Unclear battery details, no spare parts list, weak warranty, unclear model differences.

Compare RC Quotes

Tools & Machinery Supplier Review

Products: Workshop tools, commercial equipment, machinery, accessories, spare parts.

Questions: Voltage, capacity, operating manual, spare parts, warranty, service support, packing.

Documents: Technical data, manual, certificates where relevant, packing details, proforma invoice.

Red flags: No technical sheet, unclear power requirements, no spare parts, certificate mismatch.

Check Machinery Supplier

Women’s Products Sourcing

Products: Fashion accessories, bags, gift sets, retail display items, lifestyle products.

Questions: Materials, stitching, hardware, colors, logo method, packaging, defect policy.

Documents: Material details, color card, logo samples, product photos, packing list.

Red flags: Inconsistent photos, unclear materials, MOQ changes, no sample proof.

Start Private Label

Beauty & Lifestyle Product Sourcing

Products: Beauty tools, diffusers, wellness-adjacent products, gifts, lifestyle accessories.

Questions: Ingredients or materials, safety documents, packaging, label language, sample policy.

Documents: Spec sheet, safety documents where relevant, packaging dielines, certificate questions.

Red flags: No ingredient clarity, unsupported compliance claims, vague label details.

Submit Beauty RFQ

Home & Lifestyle Product Sourcing

Products: Home accessories, organizers, decor, lifestyle gifts, retail-ready private-label products.

Questions: Materials, finishes, sizes, packaging, warranty, customization, sample timing.

Documents: Product files, packing data, label details, quote, sample photos, warranty terms.

Red flags: No packaging confirmation, vague customization promises, weak product evidence.

Prepare Home Brand Brief

Custom Product Sourcing

Products: Buyer-defined products, promotional items, project needs, replacement products, new categories.

Questions: Function, dimensions, materials, target buyer, budget, MOQ, sample goals, documents.

Documents: Supplier profile, quote, product files, certificates where relevant, packing details.

Red flags: Unrealistic pricing, no proof of similar production, pressure to pay quickly.
