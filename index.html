How it works

From supplier quote to a cleaner sourcing decision.

Start with what you already have. Trillions turns product links, supplier chats, quotations, and document claims into organized questions and decision points.

1. Send your product or supplier materialShare links, photos, catalog pages, quotes, MOQ, destination market, and target price.

3. Prepare the next supplier messageGet practical follow-up questions, document requests, private-label notes, or import-readiness items.

Supplier red-flag checklist

Warning signs to review before paying a supplier.

Quote gapsMissing specs, unclear MOQ, no incoterms, vague warranty, changing price, or no sample policy.
Document gapsNo business license, inconsistent certificates, missing product files, no packing data, or unclear labeling requirements.
Communication gapsPressure to pay quickly, weak answers, no factory evidence, copied photos, or refusal to confirm details in writing.
