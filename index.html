Supplier red-flag checklist

Warning signs to check before payment.

Supplier identity gapsDifferent names, no clear website, copied photos, unclear factory address, or weak business evidence.
Quotation gapsMissing specs, unclear MOQ, no packing, no incoterms, vague warranty, or payment terms that change quickly.
Document gapsNo license, no certificate details, expired documents, missing test reports, no packing data, or unclear labels.
